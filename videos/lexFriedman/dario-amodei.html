Books on AI, Scaling Laws, and Technology

  • The Alignment Problem by Brian Christian
  • Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies by Nick Bostrom
  • Human Compatible: Artificial Intelligence and the Problem of Control by Stuart Russell
  • Life 3.0: Being Human in the Age of Artificial Intelligence by Max Tegmark

Books on Physics, Biophysics, and Natural Processes

  • The Structure of Scientific Revolutions by Thomas S. Kuhn
  • Noise by Daniel Kahneman, Olivier Sibony, and Cass Sunstein

Books on Organizational and Industry Dynamics

  • Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap... and Others Don’t by Jim Collins
  • The Innovator’s Dilemma by Clayton Christensen

Books on Philosophy and Ethics in AI

  • The Precipice: Existential Risk and the Future of Humanity by Toby Ord
  • Ethics of Artificial Intelligence edited by Markus D. Dubber, Frank Pasquale, and Sunit Das

Books on Psychology and Human Interaction with AI

  • Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman
  • Algorithms to Live By by Brian Christian and Tom Griffiths

Additional Recommendations

  • Machines of Loving Grace by John Markoff
  • The Lean Startup by Eric Ries
  • Team of Teams by General Stanley McChrystal
  • The Singularity is Near by Ray Kurzweil
